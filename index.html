Why you should visit Corvin Castle ?

Corvin Castle, also known as Hunyadi Castle, is a Gothic treasure of Transylvania an architectural masterpiece, the story of the former owners and guests would have been on the front page of the scandal newspapers of the time, it had its share of action and war.

Stunning Pictures

Here you have the opportunity to take unforgettable pictures for the holiday, the best landscape or why not wedding pictures

Great Memories

Once a week or on special days, an NGO made up of several young boys and young ladies, gives life to the castle, dressed as knights and princesses, wearing armor and various weapons ranging from swords to spears and shields. They practice ancient dances or carried on duels between knights with their weapons.
